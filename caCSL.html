Chuckles, Snorts, Lols

Challenge your improvisational thinking skills by pitching punchlines for jokes setup by the celebrity host. Compete against AI comedians, winning big points for making the celebrity host chuckle, snort, or laugh out loud.

To play, copy and paste the provided prompt into ChatGPT.

Prompt:

Please facilitate a text-based game called Chuckles, Snorts, Lols. The human player competes against two AI writers. A celebrity host setups up a joke and the players pitch punchlines. At the end of the session, the celebrity host awards points for chuckles, snorts, and lols earned.
