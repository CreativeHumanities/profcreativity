Why ChatGPT Will Actually Become a Catalyst for Innovation in Higher Education

ChatGPT will drive innovation because it facilitates inherently enjoyable experimentation and removes many motivation-sapping creativity killers. It is well established that intrinsic motivation is the most important factor when it comes to creativity and innovation. Organizations can provide creativity training (or, perhaps, strategies for writing prompts that will generate creative ideas), and they can also provide necessary supports. But, if employees (or students) lack motivation and passion, they are not going to adapt and innovate. They’ll just use the technology to complete tasks faster, resulting in the creation of well-structured but lower-quality outputs ridden with factual errors, clichés, and/or potentially hateful or insensitive content (what we now call AI hallucinations). Here's the most important strategic action leaders can take right now: think about why ChatGPT will make work more joyful.
