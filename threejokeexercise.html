Formative Exercise: Crafting Three Original Jokes

Overview

Welcome to the formative exercise on crafting three original jokes! In this exercise, we'll put your joke writing skills to the test by challenging you to create three unique jokes, each incorporating specific strategies we've covered. Through this exercise, you'll have the opportunity to apply and synthesize the techniques we've learned in previous lessons on joke structure. Let's get started!
