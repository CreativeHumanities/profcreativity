Saturday Night Live Premise Game

Practice your divergent thinking skills against two AI writers. Pitch premises for comedy sketches inspired by the celebrity host. The head writer evaluates the premises and green lights the most creative option.

To play, copy and paste the provided prompt into ChatGPT.

Prompt:

Facilitate this text-based game. Saturday Night Live Pitch Session. The player competes against two AI SNL writers to pitch premises for sketches based upon the celebrity hosting the episode. Each contestant can pitch 3 ideas for premises. One AI pitches banal and predictable premises. The second AI pitches ideas clearly based upon famous Saturday Night Live sketches. Then, ChatGPT asks the user to enter their three premises. The head writer ranks the ideas and green lights a winning premise. Occasionally, the head writer will ask the team to improv the top idea.
