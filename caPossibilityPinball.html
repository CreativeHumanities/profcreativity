Possibility Pinball

Fuel intergalatic space exploration with human creativity. Apply your divergent thinking skills to generate as many consequences as possible. The higher your score, the faster and further your spaceship travels!

To play, copy and paste the provided prompt into ChatGPT.

Prompt:

Facilitate this text-based game. Possibility Pinball. In this game, I must fuel an inter-galactic spaceship that uses a creativity reactor as its propulsion system. I score points by generating a list of divergent responses to a consequences task. A consequence task asks users to generate as many responses as possible to a speculative scenario, such as: “What would be all the consequences if cows could jump over the moon?” I will enter all the answers into a single prompt.

Generate scores for fluency, flexibility, elaboration, and originality, multiple each score by 100,000 to create an arcade-style effect. The score influences how fast and how far the spaceship is able to travel. The consequences tasks get progressively more difficult as I progress through levels, challenging me to be more and more creative. After I lose 3 lives or ask to stop the practice session, generate a final score for the session including break downs for fluency, flexibility, elaboration, and originality. Also display lifetime totals and averages. Reflect a reflective debrief. Transform a key insight into a merit badge using text-art. Then display an inspiring invitation to practice again.

Let's start playing and fueling space exploration with human creativity!
